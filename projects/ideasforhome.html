Books are a Door to Imagination
Ideas for Home

What can you do with my child at home to help support their reading development and progress? Below are some ideas!

  • Read with your child! Allow them to ask questions, and ask questions as you are reading. As they engage in reading, they will become interested.
  • As your child plays, interact with them, and incorporate literary elements! Can they help with a shopping list? Want to play restuarant, where they can take your order?
  • Take them to the library. There they can have access to additional texts, and participate in additional literacy activities (such as reading challenges!)
