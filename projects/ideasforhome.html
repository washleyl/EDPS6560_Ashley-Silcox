Ideas for Home

Literacy at home is crucial for your child's reading development. So, what can you do with your child at home to help support their reading development and progress? Below are just a few ideas!

  • Read with your child! Allow them to ask questions, and ask questions as you are reading. As they engage in reading, they will become interested.
  • As your child plays, interact with them and incorporate literary elements! Can they help with a shopping list? Want to play restuarant? Have them act as a waiter or waitress and take your order!
  • Take them to the library. There they can have access to additional texts, and participate in additional literacy activities (such as reading challenges!)
  • Let them see you read! Engage in reading simply by having famiy reading time and each read your own book!
